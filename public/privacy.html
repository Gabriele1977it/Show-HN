Privacy Policy

Last updated:

⚠️ This is a starter template, not legal advice. Have a lawyer review and adapt it (and check GDPR/CCPA obligations) before you launch.

What we collect

  • Account data: your email address and a securely hashed password.
  • Your content: transcripts, audio you upload, translations, decks and review history.
  • Billing data: handled by Stripe. We store your plan and Stripe customer/subscription identifiers — not your card number.
  • Technical data: basic request information needed to operate and secure the Service.

How we use it

To provide the Service (build and review decks, sharing, reminders, stats), process subscriptions, secure accounts, and communicate about your account. We do not sell your personal data.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe, who act as a separate data controller for payment information under their own privacy policy.

Sharing

Decks are private to your workspace unless you publish them to a public link. We share data with service providers (e.g. Stripe, and any reminder webhook you configure) only as needed to run the Service, or when required by law.

Retention & your rights

We keep your data while your account is active. You can edit or delete your decks at any time and request deletion of your account and associated data. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export or delete your personal data.

Security

Passwords are hashed (scrypt), sessions are token-based, and access to a workspace requires a member key with a role. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

Contact

Privacy questions or requests? Email privacy@echodeck.app.
